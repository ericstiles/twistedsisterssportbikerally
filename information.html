Activities

:::::::::: Timed Attack Ride ::::::::::

Sponsored By The Frio Motorcyle Shop

Hey, we're racers! We love aggressive riding! But the street is no place for racing. We've come up with the next best thing: Timed Attack. A predetermined rider will ride the entire Three Sisters a day beforehand at all legal speeds, with a stop in each town to make a purchase. The total lap time will be considered the "Perfect Score".

On Saturday Riders will start out at Frio Canyon Motorcycle Shop where they will punch a time card and carry it with them on the ride of the Three Twisted Sisters making a purchase in each town along the route. After all participants have completed the loop, returned clocked out and presented their 3 receipts the closest time to the "Perfect Score" will be first place, the second and so on.

:::::::::: You Came To Race ::::::::::

Sponsored By Extreme Powersports

We know you did! Because we did too! After all, we're Racers too! We have gone pretty far to put together the very best old S'cool hand drop Drag Racing we could. Sponsored by Extreme Powersports and "The Justified Ancients of Speed".

$15 entry fee

Bikes must pass basic Tech, self closing throttle, working Brakes, no severe leaks, nothing falling off including the Rider,and last but not least tires must be safe. All at the Tech Inspectors discretion. 3/4 of a mile north of Downtown Leakey on HWY 83. We must insist ALL Racing be confined to the AirStrip! NO EXCEPTIONS!

Safety First

Keep a few things in mind:

  • This is not a Race Track! There is no runoff, no sandtraps and worst of all, NO CELL SERVICE!
  • If you crash, you will lay on the road until someone either rides to town to call for help. Or someone rides to point with service and calls. In either case you will be on the road for up to an hour!
  • There is ONCOMING traffic, STAY IN YOUR LANE!
  • NEVER CROSS THE YELLOW LINE OF DEATH!

This being said, please ride responsibly for yourself, your loved ones and the guy coming from the other direction.
